Salesman Buddy

Car dealerships in Utah often have to gather and store information about their test drivers for legal liability reasons. This process and it's management takes time and effort. We are currently in the process of building a product that can...

  1. Gather information from test drivers throught smart phones
  2. Store the information for the required time
  3. Delete the information after the required time
  4. Gather analytics about your test drivers

This allows the car salesmen to stay with the test driver longer instead of dealing with paper work and removes most of the overhead of dealing with legal liability.
